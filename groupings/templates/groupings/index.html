BoRAT - Breakout Room Allocation Tool

Simply fill in your requirements and hit 'Find Allocations' below to see your options.

BoRAT will supply breakout room allocations that guarantee no participant is paired with any other in multiple rounds.

Once you have chosen a suitable allocation you can either download the schema or parse a list of participant email addresses.

Allocations with more rounds than your query for the same number of participants will be included in your search
